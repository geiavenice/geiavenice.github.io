GUIDED EDUCATION

At GuidEd, our mission is to channel student's particular interests to create a virtuous cycle of academic and personal growth. We believe that learning can encourage a sense of self-confidence that emboldens students to express their unique, innate desire for personal growth. We will work with them to provide guidance, context, leadership (by example), and a large dose of reality.
GuidEd offers the following services:
  • SAT & SATII preparatory material
  • Weekly contact with guidance counselor.
  • Monthly video session with guidance counselor
  • Mock Interview Sessions
  • Essay Prompts regarding current events
