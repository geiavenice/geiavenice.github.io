GUIDED EDUCATION

Let's talk about college.

WHAT WE DO

Guided Education connects high school students and their families with professional college coaches who help navigate the college admissions process over video chat and email. Our mission is to understand who you are and why you want to go to college. From there, we help you find the schools that best meet your goals and show you where you stand in relation to where you want to be. No tricks - just hard work.

WORK AS A TEAM

Through our Anderson Program, we work together to supplement your high school curriculum and prepare you for college. You will have assignments each week and meet 1 on 1 with your GuidEd Coach every month to make sure you stay on track. Your GuidEd coach will also demystify your understanding of college by candidly answering your questions throughout the process.

We keep parents informed every step of the way, too! We video chat with parents every quarter and are always available to answer questions over phone and email. We know this a big decision, both financially and emotionally, and we want to make sure there are no surprises.

GUARANTEED RESULTS

We stand by our model by guaranteeing that you will be admitted to one or more of the schools on your list; otherwise you receive a full refund.
