GUIDED EDUCATION

Let's talk about college.

Guided Education connects you with college coaches who will help you navigate the college admissions process over video chat and email. Our mission is to help you understand why you want to go to college, match you with schools that can best help you meet your goals, and then tell you where you stand in relation to where you want to be.

Through our Anderson Program, we work together to prepare for the college application process, beginning in the Fall of junior year until you matriculate into the school of your choice during the Spring of senior year. We help you understand what college is and what it isn’t. We help you help yourselves.

We stand by our model by guaranteeing that you will be admitted to one or more of the schools on your list; otherwise you receive a full refund. Plans start at $89 per month.
